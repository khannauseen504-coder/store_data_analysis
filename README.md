Store Annual Report 2022 Dashboard
Project Overview

Developed an interactive Excel dashboard to analyze store sales performance for 2022. The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, customer demographics, order status, sales channels, product categories, and regional performance to support business decision-making.

Objective

The objective of this project was to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights by identifying sales patterns, customer behavior, and top-performing regions and channels.

Key Insights
Women customers generated the majority of sales revenue.
Adult customers represented the largest customer segment.
Maharashtra was the highest revenue-contributing state.
Delivered orders accounted for 95% of all transactions, indicating strong operational efficiency.
Online marketplaces such as Meesho and Myntra contributed significantly to order volume.
Ethnic wear categories were among the top-selling product segments.
Business Impact

The dashboard helps stakeholders:

Monitor sales performance effectively.
Identify high-value customer segments.
Optimize inventory management.
Improve marketing strategies.
Focus on high-performing regions and sales channels.
Project Outcome

Successfully transformed raw transactional data into an interactive business intelligence dashboard, enabling data-driven decision-making through clear visualizations and actionable insights.
